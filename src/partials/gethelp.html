How you can help

  • Volunteering

    There is always work to be done at the refugee reception centers. You can help thousands of people with your participation.

  • Material contribution

    Every money transfer to help the Ukrainian army brings us one step closer to victory.

  • Merchandise and material aid

    In specially equipped points you can both get and bring food, hygiene products and warm clothes.

  • Cryptoassets

    It is also possible to support the AFU in cryptocurrency from anywhere in the world.
